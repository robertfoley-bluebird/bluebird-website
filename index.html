About

Senior PM expertise.
Without the overhead.

I'm Robert Foley — founder of Bluebird Consulting and a product manager with 15+ years building data-driven products at companies that care about getting it right.

Before Bluebird, I was a Senior PM at Twitter's Data Platform, where I worked on the systems that power one of the world's largest real-time data firehoses. Before that, I led product at Ada (AI-driven customer support) and ecobee (AI/ML for energy management).

I started Bluebird to work directly with ambitious teams — startups and growth-stage companies that need senior product thinking but not a full-time hire. Every engagement is hands-on, every deliverable is mine.
